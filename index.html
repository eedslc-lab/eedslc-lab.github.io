RED ROCK DEMOLITION
KayC and Nick founded Red Rock Demolition more than 10 years ago after recognizing a need for a higher standard of service within the demolition industry. With over 20 years of hands-on industry experience, they built the company on the values and knowledge gained from working their way up through every level of the trade — from laborers to foremen, supervisors, operations managers, project managers, and estimators.

Red Rock Demolition was created to fill a gap in the industry by building a company that is employee-focused, safety-oriented, and driven by a clear vision for quality and professionalism. Our goal is to provide exceptional service to our customers while creating a strong culture that puts employees first. Through hard work, experience, and dedication, Red Rock Demolition continues to deliver safe, reliable, and efficient demolition solutions for every project we take on.
