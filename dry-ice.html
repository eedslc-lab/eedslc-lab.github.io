Dry Ice Blasting
Dry ice blasting is a non-abrasive, environmentally friendly cleaning method that uses compressed CO2 pellets to remove contaminants, coatings, and residue from surfaces without damaging the substrate. Red Rock Demolition uses dry ice blasting for industrial cleaning, fire restoration, mold remediation, and surface preparation projects across the Wasatch Front and beyond.
